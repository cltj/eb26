About Us

PPOP is an independent digital media organization focused on uncovering scams, exposing bad actors, and promoting transparency throughout the ASEAN region. Through investigative journalism, data-driven reporting, and public outreach, we help individuals, businesses, and communities identify fraud, misinformation, and unethical practices before they cause harm.

Our reporting covers consumer protection, financial scams, cybercrime, corporate misconduct, public accountability, and major regional developments. Alongside breaking news and investigative reporting, we publish in-depth analyses, practical guides, and verified intelligence that help our audience make informed decisions.

Accuracy, transparency, and editorial independence are at the core of everything we do. Every investigation is based on evidence, careful verification, and multiple credible sources. We clearly distinguish between factual reporting, analysis, and opinion, and we promptly correct any errors should they occur.

Beyond journalism, PPOP offers a subscription service providing subscribers with early access to investigations, exclusive reports, scam alerts, market intelligence, and specialized briefings. We also work with readers, whistleblowers, industry experts, and partner organizations to raise awareness of emerging threats through education and public outreach initiatives.

Our mission is simple: to expose deception, inform the public, and strengthen transparency across Southeast Asia. Whether investigating sophisticated fraud schemes, reporting on major regional events, or publishing timely alerts, PPOP is committed to delivering journalism and intelligence that people can trust.
